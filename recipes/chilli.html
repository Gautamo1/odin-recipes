Vegetarian Chili Recipe

Description

Vegetarian chili is a hearty and spicy dish made with various beans, vegetables, and rich spices. It's a comforting, plant-based alternative to traditional chili and perfect for a cozy meal or a gathering.

Ingredients

  • kidney beans
  • black beans
  • tomatoes
  • onions
  • garlic
  • chili powder
  • bell peppers

Steps

  1. Saute onions and garlic
  2. Add beans, tomatoes, and bell peppers
  3. Season with chili powder and cumin
  4. Simmer until thickened
  5. Serve hot with toppings of choice
