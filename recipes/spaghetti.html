Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese (Pecorino Romano and Parmesan), pancetta, and black pepper. It's quick, simple, and indulgent, with creamy texture and savory flavors.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • eggs
  • Pecorino Romano cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • pancetta
  • black pepper

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti in salted water
  2. Fry pancetta until crispy
  3. Whisk eggs and cheese together
  4. Combine pasta and pancetta with egg mixture
  5. Season with black pepper and serve
