Classic Caesar Salad Recipe

Description

The Caesar Salad is a traditional salad made with crisp romaine lettuce, crunchy croutons, Parmesan cheese, and a tangy, creamy dressing. It's a perfect side dish or light meal.

Ingredients

  • romaine lettuce
  • croutons
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Caesar dressing

Steps

  1. Wash and chop lettuce
  2. Mix with croutons and Parmesan cheese
  3. Toss with Caesar dressing
  4. Serve immediately
