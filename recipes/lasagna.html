Lasagna recipe

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that has gained immense popularity worldwide for its comforting and hearty flavors. It’s a layered pasta casserole made with sheets of lasagna noodles, rich and flavorful sauces, creamy cheeses, and often a meaty filling, although vegetarian versions are equally delicious.
